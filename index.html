Ema-Gubevini!!!

Lubabalo Gqesha| September 12, 2016

Wamkelekile apha emzini wakwaGubevu, eli likhaya lomthombo weziduko zakwamaDuna eziqokelelweyo zadityaniswa zaze zokhelwa elikhaya ukuze nabani na onomdla kuzo angaze aphinde asokole ukuzifumana nje ngam lona. Emveni kokuba uzonwabele ke eziziduko ndiyathemba kwaye nange ngqiniseko uba uyokuphuma uphilile uba ubugula, uyakubona uba ubungaboni, uzowutsho uve nokuba ubungeva kakuhle. Eziziduko ke ziqokelelwe, hayi ukuba ziphelele kwezi. Nawe emveni kokuzifunda ungadlala eyakho inxaxheba ngokuthi undazise ezo ndizishiyeleleyo, uyakukwenza ke oko ngokuthi undishiyele umyalezo kwezidilesi zingezantsi . Zidlulile ke intsuku neenyaga neminyaka ndizama ukwakha ke elikhaya lezizDuko zakwaMaduna lizimeleyo uze wandule ke ukwenjenjeya wena mntwan'omhle usebenzisane namu siliqaqambise ufake nemvelaphi yemvelaphi yemvelaphi yazo, uwonwabele ke lombongo wazo, Camagu!
